About Us

               

Habakukk Empire is a proudly Witbank-based enterprise serving two core needs: **reliable event services** and **authentic, affordable braai meals**. We are the parent company behind our event rentals (toilets, sound, fridges) and our popular food division, Kasi Hotspot. We believe in quality, consistency, and community, ensuring both your event runs smoothly and your dinner is unforgettable.

     
   
   
     

Our Mission & Values

     

As a team, we strive to **humbly provide quality equipment and reliable services** to our clients, while also delivering **flavorful braai meals at affordable rates**, even during load-shedding. Our commitment is to grow our business in events and food services while actively **uplifting and serving our local Witbank community**.

Core Values:

  • Reliability: You can count on our deliveries and services, always.
  • Community: Supporting and reflecting the local kasi culture.
  • Quality: The best equipment and the freshest meals.
